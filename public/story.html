Who we are

CLAWS — the Cornell Lunar Astronaut Wearables Solutions club — started with a simple question: what does it take to keep humans alive, productive, and connected on the surface of another world?

We are twelve subteams — Hardware, AI, Infrastructure, UX, Research, Augmented Reality, Project Management, and the Business constellation (Socials, Content, Outreach, Finance) — orbiting a shared mission.
