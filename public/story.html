Who we are

CLAWS — the Collaborative Lab for Advancing Work in Space — started with two questions: How can a group of college students advance work in space, and what does it take to keep humans alive, productive, and connected on the surface of another world?

We are ten subteams — Augmented Reality, Artificial Intelligence, Infrastructure, Hardware, UX, Research, and the Business constellation (Social, Content, Outreach, Finance) — orbiting a shared mission.
