How the night works

Your card has a constellation, a number (1–10), and a bearing (N/E/S/W). Some of you are Polaris — anchor nodes who amplify every interaction they touch.

Tap cards to each other to trigger interactions. Same-team pairings build partial constellations; opposite bearings create polar tension; matching numbers across constellations form mirror nodes.

Vote live from the Awards tab when a round opens. The winning card lights up the digital map.
